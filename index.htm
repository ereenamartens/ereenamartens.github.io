The Map

The Consciousness Framework

The Consciousness Framework is a map of our consciousness that I created through my personal journey of my consciousness exploration. The purpose of this framework is to share the many possible layers of our consciousness that traverse across multidimensionality. Just like any map, it provides a big picture view of the consciousness and the possible ways to get from one place to another.

The books (in the Products page) take a closer look at these layers, and the many paths available to get from one place to another, depending on our preferences. The intention is to provide different perspectives that anchor us, help us find our balance and move about with intention in any direction of our choosing.

The framework itself anchors around spirituality to account for abstract possibilities and exploration of what that could entail. They are meant to stretch our imagination and explore the what ifs. This is not to invoke religious or scientific debates, but to explore perspectives with an open mind by pushing the creative limits of our thoughts, the resistance it presents and further exploration of our self, beliefs and choices.

Hi, I’m Ereena Martens

Certified Energy Healer & Serenity Coach

I help heal those seeking to take control of their life from overcoming inner conflict, societal conflict, feelings of being stuck, alone and overwhelmed. I help guide you towards your inner peace for a balanced body, mind and spirit.

Born in Pakistan and raised in Canada from teenage years and currently living in Calgary Alberta, I underwent rich experiences in cultures, family, friends, education, career and spiritual journey. I am a professional engineer and after spending some years in engineering, I transitioned into enterprise architecture which led me to make strategic technological plans for mid to large size organizations. I have rich experiences navigating and thriving in multitudes of diverse, high-stress, fast-paced environments. I have used intuition as part of my foundational toolset to provide unique perspectives and approaches to my very technical career.

During my first awakening I went from experiencing uncontrolled major ups and downs in my life to intentional and deliberate growth. This helped with normalizing the intensity of life experiences that went from things happening to me, to things happening through me. I reached a state of flow and connectedness.

My second awakening led me to abundance and manifestation. Once in a flow state I was able to find my balance and go deep within myself. I used tools like hypnotherapy, meditation and breathwork to address and resolve the root causes of my internal patterns and behaviors. As I would resolve, rewire and create new neural pathways for myself, I would use my energy to create opportunities. I felt a lot of clarity, which got me to my third awakening.

Once I had realized my energetic potential, I felt a heightened sense of connectedness with people around me. I was able to feel the blocks, and get spiritual guidance on how to help clear them. I felt a sense of fulfillment for my soul’s purpose as I helped those that were seeking help with finding their footing in their life and empowering them to find their soul purpose and peace that comes from that alignment and flow.
